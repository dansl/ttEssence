### **Ashveil the Ancient** *(Legendary · Dragon)*

A dragon so old her scales have turned ash-white. She guards a ruined city, believes herself a god, and is mostly right.

| Stats | | | |
|---|---|---|---|
| **HP:** 40 | **Defense:** 16 | **Armor**: Steel Armor | |
| **STR** 10 | **DEX** 5 | **MND** 5 | **CHA** 5 |

| Weapons | Type | Damage/Action | 
|---|---|---|
| **Claws & Bite** | Melee (STR) | Two attacks: Claws (d6) and Bite (d8). May split among targets. |
| **Fire Breath** | Magic (MND) | All heroes make DEX check (Difficulty Hard). Fail: d10 fire damage. Succeed: d6 fire damage. |
| **Battle Cry** | Action (DEX) | (Difficulty Medium) All minions gain +2 to attack and defense until end of next round. |

| Special Actions | |
|---|---|
| **Phase 1: Summon Skeleton Archers** | At the start of the battle, summons (d6) Skeleton Archers that all attack on dragons turn. |
| **Phase 2: HP 20 - Defense 10** | Becomes enraged: Claw attacks deal d8 and Bite attacks deal d10. Attacks twice. |
| **Phase 3: HP 15 - Defense 8** | Takes to the sky: Only ranged and spell attacks can reach. Fire Breath always does d10 Fire Damage |
