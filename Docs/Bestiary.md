# Bestiary Examples
### Use these as a starting point for your own monsters

---

#### Goblin Scout *(Minion · Humanoid)*
**HP:** 8 &nbsp; **Defense:** 11 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +2
**STR** 1 &nbsp; **DEX** 3 &nbsp; **MND** 1

- **Rusty Dagger** — d20+DEX vs Defense. Hit: d4 damage.
- **Sneaky Stab** — If hidden or flanking, deal +d4 bonus damage on a hit. Once per combat.

*A scrappy, cowardly creature that prefers ambushes. It squeaks when hurt and flees when half its friends are down.*

---

#### Skeleton Archer *(Minion · Undead)*
**HP:** 6 &nbsp; **Defense:** 10 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +1
**STR** 1 &nbsp; **DEX** 3 &nbsp; **MND** 1

- **Bone Arrow** — d20+DEX vs Defense, ranged. Hit: d6 damage.
- **Rattle** — Once per combat — all nearby heroes make a Mind check (difficulty 10) or lose their quick action on their next turn.

*Bleached bones held together by dark magic. Fires arrows without thought or fear.*

---

#### Shadow Wolf *(Standard · Beast)*
**HP:** 22 &nbsp; **Defense:** 12 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +3
**STR** 3 &nbsp; **DEX** 3 &nbsp; **MND** 1

- **Bite** — d20+STR vs Defense. Hit: d8 damage.
- **Trip** — d20+STR vs Defense. On hit: target falls prone — their next attack roll is made with disadvantage. Recharges each round.
- **Pack Instinct** — If another wolf is adjacent to the same target, both wolves gain +2 to attack rolls against that target.

*A midnight-black wolf the size of a pony. Hunts silently and trips its prey before going for the throat.*

---

#### Bandit Captain *(Standard · Humanoid)*
**HP:** 26 &nbsp; **Defense:** 13 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +2
**STR** 3 &nbsp; **DEX** 2 &nbsp; **MND** 2

- **Longsword** — d20+STR vs Defense. Hit: d8 damage.
- **Rally!** — Once per combat — up to 2 minion allies each immediately take a free attack or move their full distance.
- **Cheap Shot** — When a hero misses their attack, make a free dagger strike as a reaction. Hit: d4 damage.

*A scarred mercenary in mismatched armor. Barks orders at minions and fights dirty when cornered.*

---

#### Cave Troll *(Elite · Giant)*
**HP:** 44 &nbsp; **Defense:** 13 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +0
**STR** 5 &nbsp; **DEX** 1 &nbsp; **MND** 1

- **Club Slam** — d20+STR vs Defense. Hit: d10 damage. Target must make STR check (difficulty 14) or be knocked back and fall prone.
- **Double Swipe** — Make two Claw attacks (d6 each) against one or two targets. Once per round.
- **Regenerate** — At the start of each of its turns, regains 4 HP. Fire or acid damage prevents regeneration until its next turn.

*A hulking grey mass of muscle and hunger. Nearly unstoppable — and it grows back injuries unless fire or acid is used.*

---

#### Swamp Witch *(Elite · Humanoid)*
**HP:** 36 &nbsp; **Defense:** 12 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +2
**STR** 1 &nbsp; **DEX** 2 &nbsp; **MND** 4

- **Hex Bolt** — d20+MND vs Defense. Hit: d8 dark damage. Target has disadvantage on their next roll.
- **Thorn Cage** — MND vs difficulty 12. On success: target is restrained in vines. Break free with STR check difficulty 14 on their turn. Once per combat.
- **Crow Swarm** — Summons 1d4 Crow Minions (HP 4, Defense 10, Peck: d4) that act on her initiative. Once per combat.

*An old woman draped in crow feathers who smells of rot and nightshade. She speaks only in riddles and considers herself merciful.*

---

#### Iron Warlord *(Boss · Humanoid)*
**HP:** 70 &nbsp; **Defense:** 16 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +3
**STR** 5 &nbsp; **DEX** 2 &nbsp; **MND** 3

- **Greatsword** — d20+STR vs Defense. Hit: d10+3 damage.
- **Shield Bash** — d20+STR vs Defense. Hit: d6 damage; target is stunned and loses their next action. Once per round.
- **Battle Cry** — All allies gain +2 to attack and defense until end of next round. Once per combat.
- **Two Actions** — *(Boss rule)* Acts twice each round.
- **Legendary React** — Once per round when hit: immediately moves up to half speed and the incoming attack deals half damage.

*A seven-foot general in blackened plate armor. He has never lost a battle — and he wants the heroes to know it.*

#### Ashveil the Ancient *(Legendary · Dragon)*
**HP:** 130 &nbsp; **Defense:** 17 &nbsp; **Initiative:** +4
**STR** 5 &nbsp; **DEX** 3 &nbsp; **MND** 4

- **Claw / Claw / Bite** — Three attacks per turn: two Claws (d8, STR) and one Bite (d10+4, STR). May split among targets.
- **Fire Breath** — All heroes in a cone make DEX check difficulty 15. Fail: 4d6 fire damage. Succeed: half damage. Recharges on 4+ on a d6 at the start of her turn.
- **Wing Blast** — All nearby creatures make STR check difficulty 14 or are knocked prone and pushed back. Once per round.
- **Legendary React** — Once per round when hit: immediately uses Fire Breath or Wing Blast before the hit resolves.
- **Phase 2 — at 2/3 HP** — Becomes enraged: Claw attacks deal +d6; Defense drops to 15. Attacks twice as a free action immediately.
- **Phase 3 — at 1/3 HP** — Takes to the sky: only ranged and spell attacks can reach her. Fire Breath recharges every turn. She is visibly wounded — and will negotiate if heroes hold firm.

*A dragon so old her scales have turned ash-white. She guards a ruined city, believes herself a god, and is mostly right.*
