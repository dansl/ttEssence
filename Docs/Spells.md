# Spells Examples
### Use these as a starting point for your own spells

**Tier 0 — Cantrips** *(0 MP)*
- **Night Light** — Conjure a small flame or light
- **Ventriloquism** — Create a sound or voice nearby
- **Gust** — Push a small object with a gust of wind
- **Telepath** — Speak telepathically to someone you can see

**Tier 1 — Minor Spells — MND 2+** *(1 MP)* 
- **Magic Missile** — deal d4+1 force damage
- **Healing Touch** — restore d4 HP to one creature you touch
- **Shield** — gain Defense +3 for 2 turns
- **Charm** — one creature treats you as a friend for 1 turn

**Tier 2 — Moderate Spells — MND 3+** *(2 MP)*
- **Fireball** — deal d6 fire damage to creature
- **Web** — trap a creature in place for 1 turn
- **Invisibility** — become unseen for up to 2 turns or until you attack
- **Ice Blast** — deal d6 ice damage to creature

**Tier 3 — Major Spells — MND 4+** *(3 MP)*
- **Lightning Bolt** — deal d8 lightning damage to a creature
- **Fly** — one creature can fly for 2 turns
- **Revive** — restore an ally to full HP, revive if knocked-out
- **Dominate** — control a creature's actions for 1 turn

**Tier 4 — Legendary Spells — MND 5+** *(4 MP)*
- **Meteor Strike** — deal d10 fire damage to all enemy creatures
- **Stop Time** — freeze all enemy creatures for 1 turn
- **Lightning Strike** — deal d10 lightning damage to all enemy creatures
- **Heal All** — Heal all allies with d10 HP
