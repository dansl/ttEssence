# Spells Examples

Use these as a starting point for your own spells.

---

| Tier 0 — Cantrips | MND Requirement | MP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Night Light** | MND 1+ | 1MP | Conjure a small flame or light. |
| **Ventriloquism** | MND 1+ | 1MP | Create a sound or voice nearby. |
| **Gust** | MND 1+ | 1MP | Push a small object with a gust of wind. |
| **Telepath** | MND 1+ | 1MP | Speak telepathically to someone you can see. |

| Tier 1 — Minor Spells | MND Requirement | MP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Magic Missile** | MND 2+ | 2MP | Deal d4 force damage. |
| **Healing Touch** | MND 2+ | 2MP | Restore d4 HP to one creature you touch. |
| **Shield** | MND 2+ | 2MP | Gain Defense +3 for 2 turns. |
| **Charm** | MND 2+ | 2MP | One creature treats you as a friend for 1 turn. |

| Tier 2 — Moderate Spells | MND Requirement | MP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fireball** | MND 3+ | 3MP | Deal d6 fire damage to creature. |
| **Web** | MND 3+ | 3MP | Trap a creature in place for 1 turn. |
| **Invisibility** | MND 3+ | 3MP| Become unseen for up to 2 turns or until you attack. |
| **Ice Blast** | MND 3+ | 3MP | Deal d6 ice damage to creature. |

| Tier 3 — Major Spells | MND Requirement | MP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lightning Bolt** | MND 4+ | 4MP | Deal d8 lightning damage to a creature |
| **Fly** | MND 4+ | 4MP | One creature can fly for 2 turns |
| **Revive** | MND 4+ | 4MP | Restore an ally to full HP, revive if knocked-out. |
| **Dominate** | MND 4+ | 4MP | Control a creature's actions for 1 turn. |

| Tier 4 — Legendary Spells | MND Requirement | MP Cost | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Meteor Strike** | MND 5+ | 5MP | Deal d10 fire damage to all enemy creatures. |
| **Stop Time** | MND 5+ | 5MP | Freeze all enemy creatures for 1 turn. |
| **Lightning Strike** | MND 5+ | 5MP | Deal d10 lightning damage to all enemy creatures. |
| **Heal All** | MND 5+ | 5MP | Heal all allies with d10 HP. |
