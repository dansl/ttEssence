# Item Examples

Use these as a starting point for your own items. Items can be bought in shops, received as loot, or rewarded by NPCs.

---

## Consumables

Consumables are single-use items that are used up when activated.

| Name | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Health Potion | 2g | Restore 5 HP to yourself or a creature you touch. |
| Strong Health Potion | 4g | Restore 10 HP to yourself or a creature you touch. |
| Mana Potion | 2g | Restore 5 MP. |
| Strong Mana Potion | 4g | Restore 10 MP. |
| Antidote | 2g | Cure one poison or disease effect. |
| Smoke Bomb | 2g | Fill an area with thick smoke for 2 turns — all attacks in or through it have disadvantage. |
| Flashbang Stone | 2g | Throw at a creature — they must make a MND check (difficulty 12) or lose their next action. |
| Rope (10m) | 1g | Useful for climbing, binding, or puzzle-solving. GM decides what rolls it helps with. |
| Ammo (10) | 3g | Arrows, pebbles, whatever you need. Always good to have some extra! |

---

## Held Items

Held items take up a hand slot and must be actively carried to benefit from them.

| Name | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Torch | 1g | Lights a small area for 1 hour. Can be used as a weapon (d4 fire damage, no requirement). |
| Shield | 4g | Gain +2 Defense while held. Requires STR 3+. |
| Lucky Charm | 5g | Once per session, reroll any one dice roll and take the higher result. |
| Thieves' Tools | 3g | Required to attempt picking locks or disarming traps. |
| Spyglass | 4g | See clearly at long distances. +2 to any check involving spotting or scouting. |
| Grapling Hook | 4g | Let's you reach most unreachable locations with ease. |

---

## Worn Items

Worn items are equipped to the body and always active while worn. A hero can wear one of each type (head, body, hands, feet).

| Name | Slot | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hood of Shadows | Head | 4g | +1 DEX on stealth checks. |
| Circlet of Clarity | Head | 4g | +1 MND on knowledge and spell checks. |
| Boots of Speed | Feet | 4g | +1 DEX on stealth checks. |
| Gloves of Grip | Hands | 4g | +1 STR on stealth checks. |
| Cloak of Warmth | Body | 4g | Immunity to cold weather effects. +1 on checks against ice or freezing. |

---

## Magic Items

Rare items with powerful effects. 

| Name | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Ring of Mending | 10g | Restore d10 HP to yourself without using an action. Once per battle/event. |
| Amulet of the Brave | 10g | When you are knocked out, you instead stay at 1 HP. Once per battle/event. |
| Scroll of Random Spell | 5g | Contains one spell of the GM's choice. Any hero can use it once, regardless of MND. |
| Lantern of Truth | 5g | Glows brighter near hidden doors, traps, or lies. GM reveals one hidden detail per scene. |
| Boots of the Cat | 5g | You never take fall damage from any height you could survive. |
